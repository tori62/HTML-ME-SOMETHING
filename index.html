Cara Gonzalez Welker

Stanford University

Cara Welker is a 3rd year Bioengineering student working on her Ph.D. Her research is focused on improving the gait of stroke patients using haptics.

Education

Cara was born in St. Louis, Missouri where she attended Parkway Suuth High School, graduating as Valdictorian in 2010. She attended Vanderbilt University where she received a Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering and Mechnical Engineering. After graduation, Cara spent a year in Bolivia before starting her Ph.D. at Stanford University in 2015.

Interests

Cara has a variety of interests including rock climbing, singing and playing the flute at the Catholic Student Center and working with local middle school students on STEM education. During her year in Bolivia, Cara worked at an all-girls Catholic orphanage.
